Moments by the sea

Wake up to the gentle sound of the sea and enjoy mornings filled with light and fresh air. Just a short walk from sandy beaches and surrounded by nature, Del Sole offers a calm and authentic island experience where every day feels effortless.

Comfort designed for living

Each apartment is thoughtfully arranged to provide space, comfort and simplicity. With air conditioning, private balconies and fully equipped kitchens, everything is designed to make your stay relaxed, easy and enjoyable.

A location that brings everything closer

The sandy beach is only a two-minute walk away, while local restaurants offering fresh seafood and traditional barbecue are just steps from your door. You are close to the historic old town of Rab and within easy reach of Paradise Beach in Lopar, making it an ideal base to explore the island.
